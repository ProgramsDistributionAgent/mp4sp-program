About MP4SP

MP4SP (MP4 Salvaging Program) is a lightweight tool designed to recover and repair MP4 files. Note that this website and MP4SP is still on construction/development.

Features:

  • Guaranteed to work.
  • Supports a wide range of MP4 formats
  • User-friendly interface

Known Issues:

  • Weird side effects such as glitched imagery or missing sections of the media.
  • Using MaxResolutionMode will cause encoding errors and framerate drops.
  • Unstable build.
